**NOT ALLOWED IN PEDIATRICS**
- Tetracycline -> permanent discoloration of teeth
- Quinolones -> premature closure of epiphysis
- A/C -> not oral < 1yr -> diarrhea
- Chloramphenicol -> B.M. depression
- IM inj -> < 1yr


**GENERAL NOTES**
- Act on **C.W.** -> penicillins, chephalosporines.
- Act on **Nucleus** -> macrolids, aminoglycosides.
- Any ab start action after 48 hrs
- Remodeling RS Theory: When switching ab leave free for 24 hrs
